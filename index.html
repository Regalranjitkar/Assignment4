Goal is an international association football news website
founded in 2004 by Chicco Merighi and Gianluigi Longinotti-Buitoni.
It is currently owned by Footballco. Goal is the world's largest
online football publication, and second largest online sports publication,
only behind ESPN. Goal represents one of the largest football communities
in the world with 19 language versions covering more than 50 countries.
The website provides users with live scores and the latest news from football
clubs through its interactive website, mobile apps and social channels.wikipedia
